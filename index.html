2015-2016 ECA Sessions

  • Session 1: 31 August - 30 October 2015

  • Session 2: 2 November 2015 - 29 January 2016

  • Year Level ECA Sign Up Protocols

EY2

Sign up limited to a maximum total of 2 ECA activity days. (1 ECA on each activity day)

Yr 1

Sign up limited to a maximum total of 3 ECA's

Yr 2 - 13

  • Free online ECA's sign up limited to a maximum total of 2 ECA's
  • Paid online ECA's sign up is unlimited.
  • All Sports Programs sign up is unlimited.

Example: Molly in Yr7 has signed up online for board games and cooking which are free and is on the swimming team, Molly's ECA Schedule - Mon, Tues, Fri Swimming practice, Weds board games and Thurs cooking club.

A note - if a child has signed up for more than the maximum number of free ECAs the server will nullify all of the students signs up options at the end of the sign up period.

New Online Payment Option for ECA's

There is an online payment system for your ECA selections. You will have the option to pay online at the end of the sign-up process.
